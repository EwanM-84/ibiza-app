# Hero Images

Place your hero images in this directory with the following naming convention:

- `hero-1.jpg` - Primary hero image (smiling locals, community vibe)
- `hero-2.jpg` - Secondary hero image (lush mountains, coffee farms)
- `hero-3.jpg` - Tertiary hero image (colonial houses, rural scenes)

## Image Specifications

- **Dimensions**: 1920x1080px minimum (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File size**: Keep under 500KB for optimal loading
- **Style**: Realistic photos (NOT illustrations)
- **Content**: Authentic Colombian rural scenes with people, nature, and architecture

## Recommended Prompt for AI Image Generation

Use this prompt with Midjourney, DALL-E, or Leonardo.ai:

```

wide landscape shot of Colombian countryside,
authentic local Colombian hosts smiling and welcoming visitors,
lush green Andean mountains in background with scattered clouds,
colorful colonial-style houses with terracotta roofs,
warm golden hour sunlight casting long shadows,
vibrant natural greens, yellows, and earth tones,
people wearing traditional clothing mingling with tourists,
coffee plants visible in middle ground,
genuine human connection and community vibe,
professional travel photography style,
sharp focus, natural lighting, candid moment,
similar aesthetic to WithLocals homepage,
no text overlay, no illustrations, photorealistic only,
8k resolution, professional DSLR quality
--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6
```

## Alternative: Stock Photos

Find suitable images at:
- Unsplash: Search "Colombia rural community" or "Colombian coffee farmers"
- Pexels: Free high-quality images
- Shutterstock: Premium Colombian tourism photos

Edit `/components/HeroSlider.tsx` to modify timing or add more images.
