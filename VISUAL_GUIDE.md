## ๐จ Design Elements Used Throughout

### Color Scheme
- **Red (#DC143C)**: Buttons, accents, active states
- **Yellow (#FFC72C)**: Badges, highlights, secondary accents
- **White**: Card backgrounds, main background
- **Gray tones**: Text, borders, subtle backgrounds

### Component Styles
- **Rounded corners**: `rounded-xl`, `rounded-2xl`, `rounded-full`
- **Shadows**: `shadow-ios` (subtle), `shadow-ios-lg` (pronounced)
- **Padding**: Generous spacing for iOS feel
- **Borders**: Light gray `border-sptc-gray-200`

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, large (`text-3xl`, `text-2xl`)
- **Body**: Regular weight (`text-base`)
- **Labels**: Medium weight (`font-medium`)
- **Small text**: `text-sm`, `text-xs`

### Icons
All icons from Lucide React:
- Calendar, Users, MapPin, Search
- Home, DollarSign, CheckCircle, Clock
- Upload, Camera, Shield, User
- TrendingUp, AlertCircle

### Interactive States
- **Hover**: Subtle background change
- **Active**: Red background with white text
- **Focus**: Red ring around inputs
- **Disabled**: Grayed out, cursor not-allowed
